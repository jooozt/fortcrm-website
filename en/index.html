Frequently asked questions

Any questions?

What is a CRM? +

A CRM, Customer Relationship Management system, is software where you keep all your customer contacts, deals and appointments in one central place. That way everyone on your team always has the latest status at hand.

How fast can we go live? +

Most implementations go live within a few weeks, depending on the complexity of your current processes.

Do you only work with Pipedrive? +

Pipedrive is our foundation because it's fast, reliable and affordable. Where another system fits better, we'll work with that. We also have a broad network of partners who can help with specific needs.

We already have a CRM, can that be migrated? +

In most cases, yes. We first assess your current data and processes, and set up a tailored migration path.

Is this also suitable for non-profits or membership organisations? +

Definitely. This is actually one of our focus groups. We understand the different dynamic of members and volunteers compared to customers.

Do you offer discounts for social enterprises? +

We prefer to see social impact as a shared commitment. Discounts for social enterprises are always open for discussion, get in touch to see what we can do for each other.

How is our data secured? +

The technical foundation, EU hosting and GDPR tooling, is already handled by Pipedrive. Just as important is how you use it yourself: we coach your team in security best practices, so that foundation is put to good use.

Why do I need a new CRM to work with AI? +

AI is only as good as the data it gets to work with. Structured process and customer data are essential for that. A clean start with a well-integrated system is the foundation for reaping the benefits of AI.

Do you build AI workflows yourselves? +

Yes, we build AI workflows ourselves. If it gets really complex, we bring in specialized partners or refer you onward.
